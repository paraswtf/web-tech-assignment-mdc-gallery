With this goal, the National Education Policy (2020) focuses on providing holistic, comprehensive education and emphasises interdisciplinary and multidisciplinary studies. Following the NEP guidelines, the UGC has mandated nine credits of multidisciplinary courses for undergraduate education - 3 courses of 3 credits each. SIU has planned to leverage its expertise across institutes/ disciplines, with each institute offering courses to a group of students from various disciplines. By sitting together in the same classroom for these courses, the students will have a genuinely multidisciplinary experience at the college level.
